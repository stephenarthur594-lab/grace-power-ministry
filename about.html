About the Ministry

Learn more about Grace & Power Ministry and our leadership.

Prophet Saviour Koduah Duho

Prophet Saviour Koduah Duho is the founder and senior pastor of Grace & Power Ministry. He is called by God with a strong mandate for prayer, deliverance, and teaching the Word of God with clarity and power.

Through his ministry, many lives have been transformed by the grace and power of God.

Our Vision

To raise men and women of grace and power who will impact their generation through the Word of God, prayer, and service.

Our Mission

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ, equip believers, and demonstrate the power of God through love, prayer, and discipleship.
